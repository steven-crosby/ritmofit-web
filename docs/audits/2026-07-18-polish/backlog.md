# Ritmo Studio ranked polish backlog

Run: `2026-07-18-polish`

Source critique: `critique.md`

## 1. Polish thesis

**Ritmo Studio should move from a provider dashboard plus long-form Builder to a continuous movement-score
workbench by making music selection, audition, placement, and track scoring locally available at every
step.**

## 2. Ranking rules

1. Faster class build
2. Pitch-deck gorgeous
3. Live pride

Builder stays airier and consumer-music scannable—not a dense DAW. Live stays 80% safety/glanceability
and 20% swagger. The Builder path is the P0 factory; Classes, Music, Login, and Account support entry and
trust. Duration remains the only hard Live gate unless the owner separately reopens product behavior.

## 6. Out of scope / kill list

- No shell IA redesign; Classes / Music / Live / Account remain.
- No Explore, Teams, sharing, public classes, community discovery, pricing, or marketing redesign.
- No audience/room Live implementation or mockup in this program.
- No schema or migration work unless a later implementation brief proves unavoidable and the owner
  explicitly reopens it.
- No Spotify-derived BPM, provider audio caching/proxying/analysis, or derivative audio.
- No decorative Latin theming, Spanish garnish, tropical palette, or club-party gimmicks.
- No dense DAW mode, waveform editor, mixer, crossfade, or audio-production controls.
- No palette replacement for its own sake; current semantic tokens pass and support the mission.
- No requirement that BPM/cues/moves become hard Live gates; improve truth and warning hierarchy first.
- No mega redesign PR.

## 7. Open questions for owner

1. **Music selection direction (P0-01):** should Likes and playlists start with nothing selected
   (recommended), or offer a clearly labeled “select first N” convenience?
2. **Mobile scoring direction (P0-03):** should a selected track open a bottom sheet, an inline expansion,
   or a focused full-screen editor? This needs mockup comparison before implementation.
3. **Readiness vocabulary (P0-04):** recommended hierarchy is **Can start** (duration gate), **Music
   checked** (provider preflight), and **Class complete** (tempo + choreography). Confirm those terms or
   supply preferred voice.
4. **Live incomplete state (P0-05):** should a no-cue class lead with track identity + section/effort
   (recommended), or a derived neutral prompt such as “Ride the track” when no authored cue exists?
5. **Mockup scope:** approve mockups for P0-01, P0-02, P0-03, and P0-05 only; all other items are
   implement-without-mockup polish after written approval.
